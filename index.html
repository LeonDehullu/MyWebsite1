ABOUT ME

My name is Leon Dehullu, I graduated from secondary school (VTI Waregem), where I studied industrial sciences, and gained a strong passion foundation in science and technology. 
Throughout my studies, I developed a keen interest in problem-solving and a passion for developing innovative solutions to complex challenges.

I am currently in my second year of industrial engineering (industrial design) at UGent campus Kortrijk. I believe the key to succes is to have a deep understanding of the user's needs, and developing products that are both functional and user-friendly.
