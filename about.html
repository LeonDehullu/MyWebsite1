My story

My name is Leon Dehullu. I graduated from secondary school (VTI Waregem) where I studied industrial sciences, and gained a strong foundation in science and technology. Throughout my studies, I developed a keen interest in problem-solving and a passion for developing innovative solutions to complex challenges.

I am currently in my second year of industrial engineering (industrial design) at University of Ghent at campus Kortrijk. My academic focus is on the intersection of engineering and design, and I am passionate about using design principles to create innovative solutions. I believe that the key to success in engineering is to have a deep understanding of the user's needs, and to use that understanding to develop products that are both functional and user-friendly.
